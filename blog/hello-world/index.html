Hello World

Jan 22nd - 2019

Wakaful is a platform that combines islamic financial vehicles namely Takaful and Wakf in hopes of having a chance at redesigning the Takaful offerings to be more social. Our vision is to make Takaful products more accessible and inclusive.

Wakaful made their first public appearance at the Takaful Rendezvous 2018. One key take-away from the event was that the Insurance sector as a whole (including Takaful) is sharing the problems of the Banking world, that the financial and insurance products that were offered are not accessible and affordable.

It was great to see so many players in the industry coming together to try and innovate to bring takaful products to those who can't afford it necessary for us make Takaful. Wakaful aims to play a part in this innovation by applying smart contract to play a role in making takaful products more incluse and affordable.
